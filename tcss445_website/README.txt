Login.php	Authenticates account login of seller and buyer accounts
Logout.php	Logs the user out of their current session.

SellerProfile.php	Updates preferred name and email address of seller profile
BuyerProfile.php	Updates preferred name and email address of buyer profile

AddProducts.php		Creates products with prices and quantities,
					Reads from products database and displays the table of products

ProductManagement.php	Updates products database with altered prices and quantities 
						Updates products database by posting/unposting availability
						Reads from products database and displays the table of products
						Deletes the products from the products database

ShoppingCart.php	Reads from products database
					Adds products from products database into shoppingcart database
					Deletes the products from the shoppingcart database

Search.php	Reads from products database and displays relevent products
